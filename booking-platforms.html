Key Architecture Decisions
▶ Stripe Connect Onboarding
Required per salon for proper money flow
Every salon onboards to our Stripe Connect account once during setup. Without it, deposits go into our bank (and we owe the salon). With Connect, money flows: customer → Stripe Connect → salon's bank. We take a platform fee if desired. This pattern works universally across all 8 platforms.
▶ Card-on-File Visibility Mismatch
5 of 8 platforms can't see saved cards
For closed platforms (GlossGenius, Phorest, Vagaro, Booksy, Fresha), the card lives in our Stripe. Salon staff cannot see or charge it from their platform UI. We must build:
  • "Charge no-show fee" button in salon's Zoca dashboard
  • Automated no-show charging based on platform's cancel/no-show status
  • Refund flow through us (salon can't refund from their UI)
▶ Double-SMS Problem
Platform confirmation + our deposit SMS = 2 messages
Every platform sends its own confirmation SMS/email. When we also send a deposit/COF link, the customer gets two messages. Mitigations:
  • Send our deposit SMS before the platform's confirmation fires
  • Keep our SMS short and complementary, not redundant
  • For Phorest, the ~10 min confirmation delay gives us a natural window
▶ Unified Booking Flow
3-stage: live instant → hold+SMS → async post-call
Stage 1 — Live Call Instant: Search, availability, book no-deposit → API if available
Stage 2 — Hold + SMS: Reserve/hold slot, SMS customer deposit/COF link, agent stays briefly or ends call
Stage 3 — Async Post-Call: Stripe webhook fires → confirm booking → platform sends native confirmation
